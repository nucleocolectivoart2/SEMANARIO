# **App Name**: el Centro Vive y Resuena con el Semanario

## Core Features:

- Agenda Cultural Display: Browse and view the monthly cultural programming, activities, and events happening in the center of Medellín.
- Multimedia Content Integration: Access integrated content including embedded YouTube videos (14 videos as requested) and curated audio podcasts related to culture, urban memory, and heritage.
- Digital Archive & Timeline: Explore a digital record of past cultural activities, events, and contents, with an optional timeline view for chronological exploration.
- Interactive Memory Map: An interactive map powered by Leaflet.js, featuring emblematic places in Medellín's center, linking directly to related videos, podcasts, and archived activities.
- Content Management System (CMS): A streamlined interface for administrators to easily add, update, and manage cultural agenda entries, multimedia content, map locations, and archive data stored in Firestore.
- AI Content Curation Tool: A Gemini-powered tool to assist administrators in structuring, categorizing, and suggesting connections for cultural agenda content, relevant map locations, and timeline entries.
